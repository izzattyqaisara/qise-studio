My Journey

Education & Work

A little timeline of how my education, technical experience, and business journey shaped the way I build digital solutions today.

2019 – 2022 Bachelor in Computer Science

Graduated from Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS with a Bachelor (Hons) in Computer Science, majoring in Software Quality with a minor in Corporate Management.

2021 – 2022 Automated QA Engineer Trainee

Started my industry experience at Setel Ventures under the Fuel Team, where I worked on manual testing, automation testing, API validation, defect documentation, and collaboration using tools such as Selenium, TestNG, JIRA, Confluence, Swagger, Postman, Git, Maven, and IntelliJ.

2023 – 2024 Master in Computer Science

Continued my studies at Universiti Malaysia Sarawak with a Master in Computer Science. My research project focused on sentiment-based analysis of student feedback and its impact on academic performance using Python, TextBlob, NLTK, and Pearson Correlation Coefficient.

2023 – 2025 D&I Digital Drilling Analyst

Secured an experience at Schlumberger Asia Support as a D&I Digital Drilling Analyst, where I focus on data accuracy, report optimization, system requirement analysis, tool integration, documentation, and onboarding.
